\# LuxeForge Studio



\## Vision



LuxeForge Studio is an open-source parametric CAD engine for designing luxury accessories inside Blender.



Rather than importing existing models, LuxeForge Studio creates geometry procedurally using mathematical descriptions.



Our mission is to make professional luxury accessory design accessible to creators, makers and 3D printing enthusiasts.



\---



\## Core Principles



\### Parametric First



Everything is generated from parameters.



Nothing is manually modeled.



\---



\### CAD Inspired



The engine follows professional CAD concepts.



Profiles



↓



Paths



↓



Solids



↓



Meshes



\---



\### Printable by Design



Every generated object should be suitable for manufacturing.



Focus areas:



\- clean topology

\- manifold meshes

\- configurable wall thickness

\- reliable STL export



\---



\### Modular



Every component is independent.



Examples:



\- Body

\- Flap

\- Chain

\- Hardware

\- Logo



Components can be reused between products.



\---



\### Future Proof



The engine should not be limited to handbags.



Future supported products include:



\- Wallets

\- Cosmetic Cases

\- Phone Cases

\- Jewellery Boxes

\- Luggage



\---



\## Long Term Goal



Create the world's best open-source luxury accessories CAD engine.
